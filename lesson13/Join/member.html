What Do We Do?

Our purpose is to disseminate our history and preserve traditional building, traditional craft, and intangible cultural heritage. To preserve our traditon, we will disseminate advantageous information to the young generation and help them be interested in their hometown. To reach our purpose, we inform our events, history, and provide business seminar to help yong generation.

Benefits to join member

There are some benefits to be our member.

Hachinohe Chamber of Commerse offers opportunities each monthe to requruters to meet job seekers. Since our purpose is to help young generations, this opportunities will be provided both requruters and job seekers who are members.

You need to build a network of people who are thinking of you first when you encounter a situation which you need professional skills. Our network is not only local business, but also some university and high school is part of our member.
